Google+ API-Key
The Google+ API currently provides read-only access to public data.


Login to Google APIs Console with your Google account to obtain your Google+ API key. Also you can check this tutorial.
Helper for Google+ Chrome extension uses the Google+ API to retrieve public posts (including full name and profile pictures of their authors) from your stream. Absolutely no data is being sent to or processed by third party servers.

Helper for Google+ partially stores your bookmarked posts on your local machine as Chrome Bookmarks. Other your Google+ information and activity as well as aggregated statistics neither stored nor shared with publishers, app developers, or connected sites.
